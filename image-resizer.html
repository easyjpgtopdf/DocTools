Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, WebP, and all major image formats.

Will image quality be affected?

Our tools are designed to maintain high quality. You can choose quality settings to balance size and quality.

Free users can upload images up to 10MB. Premium users can process images up to 100MB.

Can I process multiple images?

Yes, premium users can process multiple images simultaneously for batch operations.
