About PDF Watermark Tool

Our PDF watermark tool allows you to add custom text or image watermarks to your PDF documents online for free. Protect your documents, add branding, or mark them as confidential with professional watermarks. The tool supports customizable text watermarks with adjustable font size, color, opacity, rotation, and position. You can also add logo watermarks for branding purposes. Watermarks can be applied to all pages or just the first page of your PDF. The tool processes files securely in your browser without uploading to servers, ensuring your documents remain private. All watermarking happens instantly with no watermarks on the output file. Perfect for protecting confidential documents, adding company branding, marking drafts, or copyright protection.

How It Works

Our PDF watermark tool uses advanced PDF-lib technology to process your documents directly in your web browser. Here's how it works:

  1. Upload: Drag and drop or select your PDF file (up to 50MB)
  2. Customize: Enter your watermark text, choose font size, color, opacity, rotation, and position
  3. Optional Logo: Upload an image file (PNG/JPG) to add as a logo watermark
  4. Select Pages: Choose to apply watermark to all pages or just the first page
  5. Process: The tool uses PDF-lib to embed watermarks directly into the PDF structure
  6. Download: Get your watermarked PDF instantly - no waiting, no server storage

All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript, ensuring your documents never leave your device.
