How It Works

Our image resizer uses advanced browser-based image processing to resize your images directly in your web browser. Here's how it works:

  1. Upload: Drag and drop or select your image file (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP)
  2. Choose Method: Select resize by pixels, percentage, or preset size
  3. Set Dimensions: Enter width and height, or use percentage slider
  4. Options: Choose to maintain aspect ratio or customize freely
  5. Process: The tool uses Canvas API to resize image in browser
  6. Download: Get your resized image instantly - no waiting, no server storage

All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript, ensuring your images never leave your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and all major image formats. You can resize any image format without conversion.

Will image quality be affected when resizing?

Our tool uses high-quality resizing algorithms to minimize quality loss. When enlarging images, some quality loss may occur, but resizing smaller generally maintains excellent quality.

What is the maximum file size?

Free users can resize images up to 10MB. Premium users can process images up to 100MB with faster processing.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Yes, premium users can resize multiple images simultaneously for batch operations. Free users can process one image at a time.

Are my images secure?

Yes, all images are processed securely in your browser and automatically deleted after processing. We never store or share your images.

Can I maintain aspect ratio while resizing?

Yes, you can choose to maintain aspect ratio automatically, or customize width and height independently for free-form resizing.
