Troubleshooting Common Issues

Issue: Images Not Uploading

Solution: Check that your image files are in supported formats (JPG, JPEG, PNG). Ensure file sizes are within the 15MB limit per image. Clear your browser cache and try again. If the problem persists, try using a different browser or check your internet connection.

Issue: Poor PDF Quality

Solution: Use high-resolution source images for better PDF quality. Check the quality settings in the conversion options. Ensure you're not compressing images too much before uploading. For printing purposes, always use the highest quality setting available.

Issue: Conversion Taking Too Long

Solution: Large batch conversions may take longer. Try processing fewer images at once. Check your internet connection speed. Close other browser tabs to free up system resources. For very large batches, consider splitting them into smaller groups.

Issue: PDF File Too Large

Solution: Use our PDF compression tool to reduce file size after conversion. Alternatively, reduce image resolution before uploading, or use medium quality settings during conversion.

Issue: Wrong Page Order

Solution: Use the drag-and-drop feature to rearrange images before conversion. Images are converted in the order they appear in the upload list. Make sure to organize them in your desired sequence before clicking the convert button.
