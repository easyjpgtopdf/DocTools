KYC & Compliance Support

Follow these steps to complete Razorpay and PayU verification for easyjpgtopdf. Keep copies of your identity and business proofs handy for a smooth approval.

Essential Documents

Identity Proof

PAN card of proprietor or business entity. Name must match bank records.

Address Proof

Aadhaar, utility bill, or rent agreement showing the operating address.

Bank Proof

Cancelled cheque or bank statement of the settlement account.

Business Proof

Shop & Establishment, MSME certificate, or other registration document (if available).

Razorpay KYC Flow

  • Update Business Website with https://easyjpgtopdf.com and keep the shipping/refund pages live.
  • Enter Business Model as self-developed digital services.
  • Upload PAN, address proof, and bank proof. Ensure scans are clear and under 5 MB.
  • Provide customer support details: support@easyjpgtopdf.com and +91-00000-00000.
  • Submit and track status under Settings → Account & KYC. Razorpay typically reviews within 24–48 hours.

PayU Onboarding Checklist

  • Register at payu.in using the same business email.
  • Fill the merchant profile with the shipping, billing, and refund URLs from easyjpgtopdf.
  • Upload PAN, Aadhaar, bank proof, and any business registration you have (shop license/MSME).
  • Set notify URL to https://easyjpgtopdf.com/api/payments/payu/callback.
  • After activation, switch keys from sandbox to live in your deployment environment variables.
