Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 November 2025

easyjpgtopdf is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use our PDF and image tools.

Information We Collect

  • Files you upload: Documents and images you select stay in your browser memory. We do not transmit or store them on our servers.
  • Usage data: We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand which tools are used most, without storing IP addresses or personal identifiers.
  • Support messages: If you email us at easyjpgtopdf@gmail.com, we keep the correspondence to resolve your issue.
  • Cookies and local storage: Some features (such as remembering preferences or recent files) use local storage on your device only.

How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our PDF and image conversion services.
  • Respond to customer support requests and provide helpful updates.
  • Monitor aggregated usage patterns to keep the site reliable and secure.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse of our platform.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers for analytics, error tracking, and advertising compliance. These partners are required to follow data protection best practices and cannot use your information for their own purposes.

Ads served through Google AdSense or similar networks may use cookies to deliver relevant content. You can manage or opt out of ad personalization through your Google account or device settings.

Document Security

  • Your files are processed locally in your browser whenever technically possible.
  • When cloud conversion is required, files are transmitted securely and deleted automatically after processing.
  • We never sell or share your documents or personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can request deletion of any support correspondence by contacting us.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser; however, some features may not function correctly.
  • If you are located in a region with specific privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may exercise your rights by emailing privacy@easyjpgtopdf.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or changes in regulations. When we do, we update the effective date above and provide notice on our website.
