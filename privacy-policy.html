How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our PDF and image conversion services.
  • Respond to customer support requests and provide helpful updates.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers for analytics, error tracking, and advertising compliance. These partners are required to follow data protection best practices and cannot use your information for their own purposes.

Document Security

  • Your files are processed locally in your browser whenever technically possible.
  • When cloud conversion is required, files are transmitted securely and deleted automatically after processing.
  • We never sell or share your documents or personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can request deletion of any support correspondence by contacting us.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser; however, some features may not function correctly.
  • If you are located in a region with specific privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may exercise your rights by emailing easyjpgtopdf@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
